Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 14, 2026

Zip Logic Game ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

We collect minimal information required for the game’s functionality:

  • Account Information: Your email address and a chosen username.
  • Authentication: Secure tokens used for passwordless login (Magic Links/OAuth).
  • Game Data: Your fastest completion times, daily streak count, and total wins.

2. How Data is Used

Your data is used solely to:

  • Maintain your daily streaks and account progress.
  • Display your rank on the global leaderboard.
  • Authenticate your session securely via Supabase.

3. Data We DO NOT Collect

We do not track, collect, or store:

  • Your browsing history or website content outside of the extension.
  • Passwords (we use secure passwordless authentication).
  • User activity such as mouse movements, clicks, or keystrokes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase as our backend provider for database and authentication services. We do not sell or share your data with any other third parties.

5. Contact

For questions regarding this policy, please contact us via our Chrome Web Store support link.
